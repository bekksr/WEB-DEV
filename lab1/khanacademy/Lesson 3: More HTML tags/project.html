Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2–3
  • Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, milk, and egg in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan and pour a small amount of batter.
  3. Cook 1–2 minutes, flip, and cook until golden.
  4. Serve with honey, berries, or jam.

Chicken Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes (plus cooked chicken)
  • Serves: 2
  • Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked chicken 200 g
Lettuce 1 bowl
Tomatoes 1–2
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Steps:

Cut the chicken and vegetables. Put everything into a bowl, add olive oil and a little salt, then mix well. You can also add lemon juice for extra taste.

Fruit Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1–2
  • Difficulty: Very easy
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 1
Yogurt or milk 1 cup
Any berries 1/2 cup
Honey (optional) 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Put banana, berries, and yogurt (or milk) into a blender.
  2. Blend for 30–60 seconds until smooth.
  3. Taste it and add honey if you want it sweeter.
